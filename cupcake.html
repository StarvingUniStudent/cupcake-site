Golden Drizzle Delight

The Golden Drizzle Delight cupcake is a caramel lover's dream come true. This decadent treat starts with a moist, buttery vanilla cupcake infused with a swirl of rich caramel. It's topped with a cloud of velvety caramel buttercream frosting, drizzled generously with golden caramel sauce, and finished with a sprinkle of sea salt for the perfect balance of sweet and savory. Whether it’s a cozy afternoon snack or the star of your dessert table, the Golden Drizzle Delight is guaranteed to leave you craving more!
